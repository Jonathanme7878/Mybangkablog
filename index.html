Bangka & Belitung Destination

Have you ever heard about Bangka Belitung? Both places are located on the same province called Bangka Belitung. While Bangka Island has its own capital city called Pangkal Pinang and Belitung with Tanjung Pandan.

Bangka Belitung has become a popular place because it used to be a location for an Indonesian movie called “Laskar Pelangi” (which was popular at that time). Since then, Bangka Belitung has become one of the favorite destinations to visit.

Located in the west of Indonesia, Bangka Belitung has many interesting things to offer for every traveler like you. It has one of favorite places to visit. It was a place for the Laskar Pelangi movie and you will be welcomed with the unique vibes of studying just like in the movie.

Besides points of interest that you may want to visit, Bangka Belitung is an offshore area where you can find beaches easily and find local food such as seafood for that easy.
